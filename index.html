SIGNUS TATTOO

Signus Tattoo opened its doors in 2009, born from the determination of an artist who believed that tattoos should be personal, meaningful, and accessible to everyone. The studio began as a small, two-room space — built with secondhand furniture and a single work lamp — but filled with passion and purpose.

The goal was simple: to offer a place for permanent art with steady quality. The studio grew little by little through walk-ins, referrals, and returning clients. Each piece helped build trust in the community.

The studio grew through patience, hard work, and perseverance. Others brought their family or friends. People asked for names, symbols, dates, and their personal design. Each request shaped them, and it improved through constant practice. Their space stayed clean, organized, and prepared daily sessions.
