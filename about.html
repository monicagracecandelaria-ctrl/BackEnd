Our Story

SIGNUS TATTOO — where shadows shape your story & every mark is a monument of memory.

Located in the heart of Sipocot, 603 San Antonio St., North Centro, Sipocot, 4408 Camarines Sur, our studio is your space for clean, creative, meaningful tattoo artistry.

Meet the Artist

I’m Jopee M. Flores, the owner and solo artist of Signus Tattoo. I started tattooing in 2009. What first began as something I needed to learn soon became something I loved doing, and that passion eventually grew into my profession.

Every tattoo has a story, and I do my best to turn each client’s idea into a design they can be proud of. My goal at Signus Tattoo is to create tattoos that are personal, meaningful, and made with care.

Whether it’s a big piece or a minimalist design, I want every tattoo to reflect something real and important to the person wearing it.
